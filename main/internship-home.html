02 FAQ
ELIGIBILITY

We focus on individuals beyond high school with less than 1 year of formal working experience. Experience in either tech or creative industries is preferred and can range from undergrad, grad student, PhD, prior internships, as well as non-traditional higher education.

Both active students and recent graduates within 1 year of their graduation may be considered. For degree holders, enrollment in a continuing education program does not automatically render one eligible for our internship program. TCIA has sole discretion to determine whether such enrollment is a qualifying eligibility event, taking into consideration all the individual circumstances.

Yes, we welcome international students who are authorized to work in the United States.

RECRUITMENT

We look for passionate individuals who demonstrate creativity, initiative, and a strong desire to learn. Key qualities include:

  • Strong portfolio or project work
  • Collaborative mindset
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Excellent communication skills
EDUCATOR
